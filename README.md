### **The World’s First Dual-Engine AI Legal Shield**

**Legal Sentinel** is a high-fidelity, mobile-first legal auditing platform designed to democratize legal protection. By fusing Google’s deep reasoning capabilities with high-speed scouting models, we’ve created a "Sentinel" that watches your back in real-time—across physical documents, digital screens, and regional languages.

---

## 🚀 The Dual-Engine Advantage
We pioneered a **Split-Neural Architecture** to solve the "Latency vs. Precision" trade-off in legal tech:

*   **🧠 Gemini 3.1 PRO (The Auditor)**: Our high-fidelity reasoning engine. It specializes in deep-context document auditing, risk heatmap generation, and bilingual legal synthesis. When accuracy is non-negotiable, Gemini takes the lead.
*   **⚡ Mistral 7B (The Scout)**: Our latency-optimized real-time specialist. It powers the **Screen Scout** harvesting loop and the **Real-Time Voice Chat**, providing sub-second transcription and feedback for rapid legal inquiries.

---

## 🛠️ Key Innovation Pillars

### **1. Screen Scout (Real-Time Harvesting)**
A custom-built OCR harvesting engine that extracts legal terms directly from your browser or other apps. As you scroll through "Fine Print," the Sentinel captures and analyzes data in the background, ensuring you never miss a hidden trap.

### **2. Sentinel Lens (Direct Scan)**
Leveraging high-performance image processing and **Tesseract OCR**, the Lens transforms physical paper notices, parking tickets, and contracts into structured legal intelligence in seconds.

### **3. Regional Indian Engine (Bilingual Intelligence)**
The first auditor built for 1.4 Billion people. Support for:
*   **Languages**: English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali.
*   **Result**: High-fidelity, bilingual audit reports (English + Regional) for maximum official standing and personal clarity.

## 🧰 Tech Stack & Ecosystem
*   **AI Core**: Gemini 3.1 Pro (via Mistral/Gemini Proxy), Mistral 7B.
*   **Vision Engine**: Tesseract.js (Singleton Worker Architecture) for high-performance OCR.
*   **Document Parsers**: PDF.js (Vector Extraction), Mammoth.js (Word/Docx Analysis).
*   **UI/UX**: Modern Trust Design System (Pure Vanilla CSS Glassmorphism).
*   **Google Tools**:
    *   **Gemini AI**: Deep Neural Reasoning.
    *   **Google Fonts**: Outfit, Inter, and Roboto Mono for typographic hierarchy.

## 🏗️ Installation & Deployment

1.  **Clone the Repository**:
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/YourRepo/Legal-Sentinel
    ```
2.  **Environment Setup**:
    Add your `MISTRAL_API_KEY` to `src/gemini_service.js`.
3.  **Run Locally**:
    ```bash
    npm install
    npm run dev
    ```
